What is it?

Watch it is a project aimed at improving the mental health awareness of everyday people by making information that is linked to the development of mental health issues more accessible

This is done by using generic SmartWatches running an android environment and an accompanying companion app to take data such as a persons heart rate and use it to detect if they are stressed or not.

They can then use that information to improve their own mindfulness or associate a therapist so they can view the data

How to

How to use it

    1. All
    2. Wearers
    3. Therapists

Wearer

A wearer is someone who's data is collected by the system. They will need an android based SmartPhone and SmartWatch. Once they have installed the required applications they will then have to set up an account with an email and password. When the setup has been completed the app will start to record their data right away and upload it the cloud. From the phone app they can activate a meditation feature to impove their own mindfulness or associate with a therapist.

Therapist

Therapists can register and login to the system via this website. They will be provided with a "Therapist ID" once they have signed up, this ID can be given to any of their patients that use the watch it system to associate with the therapist. The therapist can then view their patient's data including such information like their hourly heart rate and how often they where stressed in the last week.

Getting started

Getting started is easy, if you wish to use the application to read your own data then just download the app from google play store for both your SmartWatch and SmartPhone.

If you are a therapist that wishes to sign up to the system then just register here and login in to see the therapist console.
